                               RESOLUTION


 
Making majority party appointments to the Energy and Natural Resources 
Committee, the Veterans' Affairs Committee, and the Committee on Indian 
                                Affairs.

    Resolved, That the following shall constitute the majority party's 
membership on the following Senate committees for the 104th Congress, 
or until their successors are appointed:
    Energy and Natural Resources: Mr. Murkowski (chairman), Mr. 
Hatfield, Mr. Domenici, Mr. Nickles, Mr. Craig, Mr. Campbell, Mr. 
Thomas, Mr. Kyl, Mr. Grams, Mr. Jeffords, and Mr. Burns.
    Veterans' Affairs: Mr. Simpson (chairman), Mr. Murkowski, Mr. 
Specter, Mr. Thurmond, Mr. Jeffords, Mr. Campbell, and Mr. Craig.
    Indian Affairs: Mr. McCain (chairman), Mr. Murkowski, Mr. Gorton, 
Mr. Domenici, Mrs. Kassebaum, Mr. Nickles, Mr. Campbell, Mr. Thomas, 
and Mr. Hatch.
