  COMMITTEE ON SMALL BUSINESS: Mr. Bumpers (Chairman), Mr. Nunn, Mr. Baucus,

  COMMITTEE ON VETERANS' AFFAIRS: Mr. Cranston (Chairman), Mr. Matsunaga,
