                               RESOLUTION


 
 Making minority party appointments to the Select Committee on Ethics.

    Resolved, The following shall constitute minority membership of the 
Select Committee on Ethics for the One Hundred Third Congress or until 
their successors are named: Mitch McConnell (Vice Chairman), Ted 
Stevens (vice, Trent Lott), and Bob Smith (vice, Slade Gorton).
