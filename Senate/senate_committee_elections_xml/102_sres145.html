To make a minority party change in committees.
IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
June 25 (legislative day, JUNE 11), 1991
Mr. GORTON (for Mr. DOLE) submitted the following resolution; which was

RESOLUTION
To make a minority party change in committees.
  Resolved, That the following Senator shall be added to the minority party's
  membership on the Committee on Finance and removed from the minority party's
  membership on the Committee on Foreign Relations for the One Hundred Second
  Congress or until their successors are appointed:
  Mr. Hatch.
