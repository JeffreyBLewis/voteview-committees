                               RESOLUTION


 
    To constitute the minority party's membership on certain of the 
standing committees for the One Hundred Third Congress, or until their 
                         successors are chosen.

    Resolved, That the following shall constitute the minority party's 
membership on the following standing committee for the One Hundred 
Third Congress, or until their successors are chosen:
    Committee on Governmental Affairs: Mr. Roth, Mr. Stevens, Mr. 
Cohen, Mr. Cochran, Mr. McCain, and Mr. Bennett.
