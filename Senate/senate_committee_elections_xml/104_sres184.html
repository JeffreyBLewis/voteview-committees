                               RESOLUTION


 
Making majority party appointments to certain Senate committees for the 
                            104th Congress.

    Resolved, That the following shall constitute the majority party's 
membership on the following standing committees for the 104th Congress, 
or until their successors are chosen:
    Committee on Agriculture: Mr. Lugar, Mr. Dole, Mr. Helms, Mr. 
Cochran, Mr. McConnell, Mr. Craig, Mr. Coverdell, Mr. Santorum, Mr. 
Warner, and Mr. Grassley.
    Committee on Banking, Housing, and Urban Affairs: Mr. D'Amato, Mr. 
Gramm, Mr. Shelby, Mr. Bond, Mr. Mack, Mr. Faircloth, Mr. Bennett, Mr. 
Grams, and Mr. Domenici.
    Committee on Commerce, Science, and Transportation: Mr. Pressler, 
Mr. Stevens, Mr. McCain, Mr. Burns, Mr. Gorton, Mr. Lott, Mrs. 
Hutchison, Ms. Snowe, Mr. Ashcroft, and Mr. Frist.
    Committee on Governmental Affairs: Mr. Stevens, Mr. Roth, Mr. 
Cohen, Mr. Thompson, Mr. Cochran, Mr. McCain, Mr. Smith, and Mr. Brown.
