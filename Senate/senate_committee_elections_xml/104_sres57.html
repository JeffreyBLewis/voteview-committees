                               RESOLUTION


 
 A resolution making majority party appointments to the Small Business 
       and Aging Committees for the One Hundred Fourth Congress.

    Resolved, That the following shall constitute the majority party's 
membership on the following Senate committees for the One Hundred 
Fourth Congress, or until their successors are appointed:
    Small Business: Mr. Bond (Chairman), Mr. Pressler, Mr. Burns, Mr. 
Coverdell, Mr. Kempthorne, Mr. Bennett, Mrs. Hutchison, Mr. Warner, Mr. 
Frist, and Ms. Snowe.
    Aging: Mr. Cohen (Chairman), Mr. Pressler, Mr. Grassley, Mr. 
Simpson, Mr. Jeffords, Mr. Craig, Mr. Burns, Mr. Shelby, Mr. Santorum, 
and Mr. Thompson.
