                               RESOLUTION


 
 Making Minority party appointments for the Committees on Environment 
   and Public Works and Governmental Affairs for the 107th Congress.

    Resolved, That the following be the minority membership on the 
Committees on Environment and Public Works and Governmental Affairs for 
the remainder of the 107th Congress, or until their successors are 
appointed:
    Committee on Environment and Public Works: Mr. Smith of New 
Hampshire, Mr. Warner, Mr. Inhofe, Mr. Bond, Mr. Voinovich, Mr. Crapo, 
Mr. Chafee, Mr. Specter, and Mr. Domenici.
    Committee on Governmental Affairs: Mr. Thompson, Mr. Stevens, Ms. 
Collins, Mr. Voinovich, Mr. Cochran, Mr. Bennett, Mr. Bunning, and Mr. 
Fitzgerald.
