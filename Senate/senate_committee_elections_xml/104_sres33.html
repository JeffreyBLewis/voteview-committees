                               RESOLUTION


 
  Making majority party appointments to Senate committees for the One 
                        Hundred Fourth Congress.

    Resolved, That the following shall constitute the majority party's 
membership on those Senate committees listed below for the One Hundred 
Fourth Congress, or until their successors are appointed:
    Budget: Mr. Domenici, Mr. Grassley, Mr. Nickles, Mr. Gramm, Mr. 
Bond, Mr. Lott, Mr. Brown, Mr. Gorton, Mr. Gregg, Ms. Snowe, Mr. 
Abraham, and Mr. Frist.
    Rules and Administration: Mr. Stevens, Mr. Hatfield, Mr. Helms, Mr. 
Warner, Mr. Dole, Mr. McConnell, Mr. Cochran, Mr. Santorum, and Mr. 
Nickles.
    Veterans' Affairs: Mr. Simpson, Mr. Murkowski, Mr. Specter, Mr. 
Thurmond, Mr. Jeffords, Mr. Craig, and Mr. Brown.
