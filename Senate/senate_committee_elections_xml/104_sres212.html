                   IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES

 Mr. Daschle submitted the following resolution; which was considered

                               RESOLUTION


 
 To constitute the minority party's membership on the Ethics Committee 
     for the 104th Congress, or until their successors are chosen.

    Resolved, That the following shall constitute the minority party's 
membership on the Ethics Committee for the 104th Congress, or until 
their successors are chosen: The Senator from North Dakota (Mr. 
Dorgan), Vice Chairman, the Senator from Nevada (Mr. Reid), and the 
Senator from Washington (Mrs. Murray).
