                               RESOLUTION


 
   To constitute the majority party's membership on the Governmental 
Affairs committee for the 105th Congress, or until their successors are 
                                 chosen

    Resolved, That notwithstanding the restrictions contained in rule 
25, the following shall be the majority party's membership on the 
Governmental Affairs committee for the 105th Congress, or until their 
successors are chosen:
    Committee on Governmental Affairs: Mr. Thompson, (Chair) Ms. 
Collins, Mr. Brownback, Mr. Domenici, Mr. Cochran, Mr. Nickles, Mr. 
Specter, Mr. Smith of New Hampshire, and Mr. Bennett.
