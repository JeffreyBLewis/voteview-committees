                               RESOLUTION


 
 A resolution making majority party appointments to Senate committees 
                        for the 105th Congress.

    Resolved, That notwithstanding the provisions of S. Res. 400 of the 
95th Congress, or the provisions of rule XXV, the following shall 
constitute the majority party's membership on those Senate committees 
listed below for the 105th Congress, or until their successors are 
appointed:
    Budget: Mr. Domenici (chair), Mr. Grassley, Mr. Nickels, Mr. Gramm, 
Mr. Bond, Mr. Gorton, Mr. Gregg, Ms. Snowe, Mr. Abraham, Mr. Frist, Mr. 
Grams, and Mr. Smith.
    Rules and Administration: Mr. Warner (chair), Mr. Helms, Mr. 
Stevens, Mr. McConnell, Mr. Cochran, Mr. Santorum, Mr. Nickles, Mr. 
Lott, and Mrs. Hutchison.
    Small Business: Mr. Bond (chair), Mr. Burns, Mr. Coverdell, Mr. 
Kempthorne, Mr. Bennett, Mr. Warner, Mr. Frist, Ms. Snowe, Mr. 
Faircloth, and Mr. Enzi.
    Veterans' Affairs: Mr. Specter (chair), Mr. Murkowski, Mr. 
Thurmond, Mr. Jeffords, Mr. Campbell, Mr. Craig, and Mr. Hutchinson.
    Select Committee on Ethics: Mr. Smith (chair), Mr. Roberts, and Mr. 
Sessions.
    Special Committee on Aging: Mr. Grassley (chair), Mr. Jeffords, Mr. 
Craig, Mr. Burns, Mr. Shelby, Mr. Santorum, Mr. Warner, Mr. Hagel, Ms. 
Collins, and Mr. Enzi.
    Select Committee on Indian Affairs: Mr. Campbell (chair), Mr. 
Murkowski, Mr. McCain, Mr. Gorton, Mr. Domenici, Mr. Thomas, Mr. Hatch, 
and Mr. Inhofe.
    Intelligence: Mr. Shelby (chair), Mr. Chafee, Mr. Lugar, Mr. 
DeWine, Mr. Kyl, Mr. Inhofe, Mr. Hatch, Mr. Roberts, Mr. Allard, and 
Mr. Coats.
    Joint Economic: Mr. Mack (vice chair), Mr. Roth, Mr. Bennett, Mr. 
Grams, Mr. Brownback, and Mr. Sessions.
