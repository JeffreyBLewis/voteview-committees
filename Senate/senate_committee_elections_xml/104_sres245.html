                               RESOLUTION


 
  Making majority party appointments to the Labor and Human Resources 
                               Committee.

    Resolved, That notwithstanding any provision in Rule 25 or 26, the 
following be the majority party membership on the Committee on Labor 
and Human Resources for the 104th Congress, or until their successors 
are appointed:
    Labor and Human Resources: Mrs. Kassebaum (Chairman), Mr. Jeffords, 
Mr. Coats, Mr. Gregg, Mr. Frist, Mr. DeWine, Mr. Ashcroft, Mr. Gorton, 
and Mr. Faircloth.
