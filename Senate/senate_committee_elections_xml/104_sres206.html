   Making minority party appointments for the Committee on Veterans' 
                                Affairs.

    Resolved, That the following shall constitute the minority party's 
membership on the Committee on Veterans' Affairs for the second session 
of the 104th Congress, or until their successors are appointed: Mr. 
Rockefeller, Mr. Graham, Mr. Akaka, Mr. Wellstone, and Mrs. Murray.
