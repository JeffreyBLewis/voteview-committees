RESOLUTION
Electing Representative Ewing to the Committee on Agriculture and the
Committee on Public Works and Transportation; Representative Zeliff to the
Committee on Small Business; and Representative Baker to the Committee on
Interior and Insular Affairs.
  Resolved, That the following named Members be, and they are hereby,
  elected to the following standing committees of the House of Representatives:
  COMMITTEE ON AGRICULTURE: Tom Ewing of Illinois.
  COMMITTEE ON INTERIOR AND INSULAR AFFAIRS: Richard H. Baker of Louisiana.
  COMMITTEE ON PUBLIC WORKS AND TRANSPORTATION: Tom Ewing of Illinois.
  COMMITTEE ON SMALL BUSINESS: Bill Zeliff of New Hampshire.
