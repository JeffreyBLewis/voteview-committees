   RESIGNATION AS MEMBER OF COMMITTEE ON EDUCATION AND THE WORKFORCE

  The SPEAKER laid before the House the following resignation as a 
member

[[Page H2294]]

of the Committee on Education and the Workforce:

                                                   April 10, 2024.
     Hon. Mike Johnson,
     Speaker, House of Representatives,
     Washington, DC.
       Dear Speaker Johnson: I write to respectfully tender my 
     resignation as a member of the Education & The Workforce 
     Committee. It has been an honor to serve in this capacity.
           Sincerely,
                                                    John E. James,
                                               Member of Congress.

  The SPEAKER. Without objection, the resignation is accepted.
  There was no objection.
