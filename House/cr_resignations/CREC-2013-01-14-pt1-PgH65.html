 RESIGNATIONS AS MEMBER OF COMMITTEES ON ARMED SERVICES AND AGRICULTURE

  The SPEAKER pro tempore laid before the House the following 
resignations as a member of the Committees on Armed Services and 
Agriculture:

                                     House of Representatives,

                                 Washington, DC, January 14, 2013.
     Hon. John Boehner,
     Speaker, House of Representatives,
     U.S. Capitol, Washington, DC.
       Dear Speaker Boehner: In order to join the Committee on 
     Appropriations, I hereby resign my seat on both the Armed 
     Services Committee and the Committee on Agriculture, 
     effective today.
           Sincerely,
                                                       Bill Owens,
                                               Member of Congress.

  The SPEAKER pro tempore. Without objection, the resignations are 
accepted.
  There was no objection.
