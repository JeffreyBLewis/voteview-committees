            RESIGNATION AS MEMBER OF COMMITTEE ON THE BUDGET

  The SPEAKER pro tempore (Mr. Linder) laid before the House the 
following resignation as a member of the Committee on the Budget:
                                    Congress of the United States,


                                     House of Representatives,

                                Washington, DC, February 12, 2001.
     Hon. Dennis Hastert,
     Speaker, House of Representatives,
     Washington, DC.
       Dear Mr. Speaker: Pursuant to my appointment to the House 
     Budget Committee, I hereby take leave of my assignment to the 
     Committee on Small Business. Thank you.
           Sincerely,
                                                 Carolyn McCarthy,
                                               Member of Congress.

  The SPEAKER pro tempore. Without objection, the resignation is 
accepted.
  There was no objection.
