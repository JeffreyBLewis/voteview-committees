              RESIGNATION AS MEMBER OF COMMITTEE ON RULES

  The SPEAKER laid before the House the following resignation as a 
member of the Committee on Rules:
                                    Congress of the United States,


                                     House of Representatives,

                                 Washington, DC, January 27, 2021.
     Speaker Nancy Pelosi,
     Capitol Building,
     Washington, DC.
       Speaker Pelosi: I write to inform you that I hereby resign 
     from the Committee on Rules.
           Sincerely,
                                                     Debbie Lesko,
                                               Member of Congress.
  The SPEAKER. Without objection, the resignation is accepted.
  There was no objection.
