          RESIGNATION AS MEMBER OF COMMITTEE ON THE JUDICIARY

  The SPEAKER pro tempore laid before the House the following 
resignation as a member of the Committee on the Judiciary:

                                    Congress of the United States,


                                     House of Representatives,

                                   Washington, DC, March 14, 1996.
     Hon. Newt Gingrich,
     U.S. House of Representatives, Washington, DC.
       Dear Speaker, I hereby resign from the House Committee on 
     the Judiciary.
       With best wishes, I am
           Sincerely,
                                                  Jose E. Serrano,
                                               Member of Congress.

  The SPEAKER pro tempore. Without objection, the resignation is 
accepted.
  There was no objection.
