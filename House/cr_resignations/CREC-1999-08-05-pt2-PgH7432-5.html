  RESIGNATION AS MEMBER OF COMMITTEE ON BANKING AND FINANCIAL SERVICES

  The SPEAKER pro tempore laid before the House the following 
resignation as a member of the Committee on Banking and Financial 
Services:

                                                 Gary L. Ackerman,


                                Congress of the United States,

                           5th District, New York, August 5, 1999.
     Hon. J. Dennis Hastert,
     Speaker, House of Representatives,
     Washington, DC.
       Dear Mr. Speaker: This letter is to inform you of that I do 
     hereby resign from the Committee on Banking and Financial 
     Services, effective immediately.
       Sincerely,
                                                 Gary L. Ackerman,
                                               Member of Congress.

  The SPEAKER pro tempore. Without objection, the resignation is 
accepted.
  There was no objection.
