        RESIGNATION AS MEMBER OF COMMITTEE ON VETERANS' AFFAIRS

  The SPEAKER pro tempore (Mr. Hansen) laid before the House the 
following resignation as a member of the Committee on Veterans' 
Affairs:
                                     Congress of the United States


                                      House of Representatives

                                    Washington, DC, June 20, 2001.
     The Speaker
     The Capitol, Washington, D.C.
       Dear Mr. Speaker: I hereby resign my seat on the Veterans' 
     Affairs Committee effective immediately.
           Best regards,
                                                   J. D. Hayworth,
                                               Member of Congress.

  The SPEAKER pro tempore. Without objection, the resignation is 
accepted.
  There was no objection.
