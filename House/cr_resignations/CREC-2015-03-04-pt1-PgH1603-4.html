  RESIGNATION AS MEMBER OF COMMITTEE ON SCIENCE, SPACE, AND TECHNOLOGY

  The SPEAKER pro tempore laid before the House the following 
resignation as a member of the Committee on Science, Space, and 
Technology:

                                                February 18, 2015.
     Hon. John Boehner,
     Speaker of the House, The Capitol,
     Washington, DC.
       Dear Speaker Boehner, This letter serves as my official 
     resignation from the House Committee on Science, Space, and 
     Technology. It has been my pleasure serving on this Committee 
     since being elected to Congress.
           Best Regards,
                                              Frederica S. Wilson,
                                               Member of Congress.

  The SPEAKER pro tempore. Without objection, the resignation is 
accepted.
  There was no objection.
