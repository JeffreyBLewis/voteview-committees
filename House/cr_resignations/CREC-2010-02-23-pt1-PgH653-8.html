       RESIGNATION AS MEMBER OF COMMITTEE ON ENERGY AND COMMERCE

  The SPEAKER pro tempore laid before the House the following 
resignation as a member of the Committee on Energy and Commerce:

                                                February 23, 2010.
     Hon. Nancy Pelosi,
     Speaker of the House, House of Representatives, The Capitol, 
         Washington, DC.
       Dear Speaker Pelosi: This letter serves as my intent to 
     resign from the Committee on Energy and Commerce, effective 
     today.
           Sincerely,
                                                      Greg Walden,
                                               Member of Congress.

  The SPEAKER pro tempore. Without objection, the resignation is 
accepted.
  There was no objection.
