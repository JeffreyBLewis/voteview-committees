            RESIGNATION AS MEMBER OF COMMITTEE ON RESOURCES

  The SPEAKER pro tempore (Mr. Tiberi) laid before the House the 
following resignation as a member of the Committee on Resources:

                                     House of Representatives,

                                                    June 14, 2004.
     Hon. J. Dennis Hastert,
     Speaker of the House, U.S. Capitol, Washington, DC.
       Dear Mr. Speaker: Effective immediately, I hereby resign my 
     seat on the Committee on

[[Page H3928]]

     Resources, pending my appointment to the Committee on 
     Government Reform.
           Sincerely,
                                                   Betty McCollum,
                                               Member of Congress.

  The SPEAKER pro tempore. Without objection, the resignation is 
accepted.
  There was no objection.
