            RESIGNATION AS MEMBER OF COMMITTEE ON THE BUDGET

  The SPEAKER laid before the House the following resignation as a 
member of the Committee on the Budget:

                                    Congress of the United States,


                                     House of Representatives,

                                 Washington, DC, December 4, 2015.
     Hon. Paul D. Ryan,
     Office of the Speaker,
     Washington, DC.
       Mr. Speaker, In light of my recent appointment as Chairman 
     of the Human Resource Subcommittee on Ways and Means, I 
     hereby resign my position on the House Budget Committee.
           Best Regards,
                                        Congressman Vern Buchanan.

  The SPEAKER. Without objection, the resignation is accepted.
  There was no objection.
