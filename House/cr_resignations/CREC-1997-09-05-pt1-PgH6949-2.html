            RESIGNATION AS MEMBER OF COMMITTEE ON THE BUDGET

  The SPEAKER pro tempore laid before the House the following 
resignation as a member of the Committee on the Budget:

                                                September 4, 1997.
     Hon. Newt Gingrich,
     The Speaker, House of Representatives, Washington, DC.
       Dear Mr. Speaker: Effective immediately I do hereby resign 
     my assignment on the Committee on the Budget to take a new 
     assignment on the Committee on Banking and Financial 
     Services.
           Sincerely,
                                                     Brad Sherman,
                                               Member of Congress.

  The SPEAKER pro tempore. Without objection, the resignation is 
accepted.
  There was no objection.
