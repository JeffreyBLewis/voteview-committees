    RESIGNATIONS AS MEMBER OF COMMITTEE ON THE JUDICIARY AND SELECT 
      SUBCOMMITTEE ON THE WEAPONIZATION OF THE FEDERAL GOVERNMENT

  The SPEAKER pro tempore laid before the House the following 
resignations as a member of the Committee on Judiciary and the Select 
Subcommittee on the Weaponization of the Federal Government:

                                     House of Representatives,

                                 Washington, DC, October 25, 2023.
     Hon. Kevin McCumber,
     Acting Clerk, House of Representatives,
     Washington, DC.
       Dear Mr. McCumber: As a result of my election today as 
     Speaker, this letter is to inform you that I resign as a 
     member of the Committee on the Judiciary. I also resign as a 
     member of the House Judiciary Select Subcommittee on the 
     Weaponization of the Federal Government.
           Sincerely,
                                                     Mike Johnson,
                                             Speaker of the House.

  The SPEAKER pro tempore. Without objection, the resignations are 
accepted.
  There was no objection.
