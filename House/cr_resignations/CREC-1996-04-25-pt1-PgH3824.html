        RESIGNATION AS MEMBER OF COMMITTEE ON VETERANS' AFFAIRS

  The SPEAKER pro tempore (Mr. LaHood) laid before the House the 
following resignation as a Member of the Committee on Veterans' 
Affairs:

                                    Congress of the United States,


                                     House of Representatives,

                                   Washington, DC, April 22, 1996.
     Hon. Newt Gingrich,
     Speaker of the House,
     Washington, DC.
       Dear Mr. Speaker: I hereby resign my position on the 
     Committee on Veterans' Affairs.
           Sincerely,
                                                    Maxine Waters,
                                               Member of Congress.

  The SPEAKER pro tempore. Without objection, the resignation is 
accepted.
  There was no objection.
