           RESIGNATION AS MEMBER OF COMMITTEE ON AGRICULTURE

  The SPEAKER pro tempore (Mr. Ose) laid before the House the following 
resignation as a member of the Committee on Agriculture:

                                                    U.S. Congress,


                                     House of Representatives,

                                     Washington, DC, June 3, 2004.
     Hon. Dennis Hastert,
     Speaker of the House, House of Representatives, The Capitol, 
         Washington, DC.
       Dear Mr. Speaker: Effective today, June 3rd, 2004, I hereby 
     resign from the Committee on Agriculture.
           Sincerely,
                                                    Mike Thompson,
                                               Member of Congress.

  The SPEAKER pro tempore. Without objection, the resignation is 
accepted.
  There was no objection.
