            RESIGNATION AS MEMBER OF COMMITTEE ON THE BUDGET

  The SPEAKER pro tempore laid before the House the following 
resignation from the Committee on the Budget:

                                    Congress of the United States,


                                     House of Representatives,

                                   Washington, DC, April 25, 1996.
     Hon. Newt Gingrich,
     Speaker, U.S. House of Representatives, The Capitol, 
         Washington, DC.
       Dear Mr. Speaker: I hereby resign from the Committee on the 
     Budget.
           Sincerely,
                                                   Harry Johnston.

  The SPEAKER pro tempore. Without objection, the resignation will be 
accepted.
  There was no objection.
