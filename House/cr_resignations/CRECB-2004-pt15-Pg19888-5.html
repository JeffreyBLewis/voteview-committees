   RESIGNATION AS MEMBER OF CERTAIN STANDING COMMITTEES OF THE HOUSE

  The SPEAKER laid before the House the following resignation as a 
member of the Committee on Agriculture, the Committee on Government 
Reform, and the Committee on the Budget:

                                     House of Representatives,

                               Washington, DC, September 28, 2004.
     Hon. J. Dennis Hastert,
     Speaker of the House, The Capitol, Washington, DC.
       Dear Mr. Speaker: I hereby resign from the House 
     Agriculture, Government Reform and Budget Committees due to 
     my appointment to the House Committee on Rules.
           Sincerely,
                                                   Adam H. Putnam,
                                               Member of Congress.

  The SPEAKER. Without objection, the resignations are accepted.
  There was no objection.
